about app

This is an app developed for The University of Alabama for the Capstone class for a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science.

Our app takes a look at habit creation. We noticed that the other habit creation apps are way over complicated and have too many bells and whistles. While this may be helpful to some, to many others the complexity will prevent someone from checking in and continuing their habit. Anyone who has trying to create a habit or destroy one knows that we as humans look for any excuse not to do the task. That's why our app focuses on creating a habit app that takes away the complexity and keeps amount of things that the user has to do to a minimum, to cut out the distractions.

app features

Here are some of the core features that are built into our app.
These focus on making the experiences as gamified as possible.

Google Play Integration

Login with your Google Games account to get the maximum experience with the app.

Achievements

Keep track of your progress with achievements. These are fully integrated with Google Games.

Cloud Integration

Never worry about losing all of your data. Your habit information is stored safely in the cloud.

Analytics

See how close you are to your objectives with analytics that can be found with each habit.
